The Mylestones Goal

My name is Tanner Kopel, and I created Mylestones for the 2022 Congressional App Challenge, an app-making challenge for high schoolers run by the United States Congress. I wanted an easy place to record the many milestones I was having in life, and I couldn't find the right app to do so. Mylestones allows you to add important moments in your life, and even turn them into a slideshow so you can remember them forever. Thanks for using Mylestones!

How It Works

Using the Mylestones app is really simple. First, create an account or login using the buttons on the top right. Once you've signed in, press the big plus in the bottom left corner to add a milestone. Fill out the form with some details about the milestone, and press "add milestone" to add the milestone to your account. If you want to show off your milestones, press the "make a slideshow" button on the bottom right and configure the settings for the slideshow. Finally, hit the "Make slideshow" button, and watch your memories come to life! If you have any questions or concerns, please email me at tanner@kopelfamily.com
